Why read through this site?

For anyone in the tech space, the Internet and World Wide Web allows you to do your job. If you are trying to get a job developing the WWW, you need to know the history, otherwise, you will not know what to learn and avoid. Daily tasks, communication, and enjoyment depend on the Internet. It allows people to share, exchange, and connect ideas, information, and news. The reason why everyone should learn about the history is due to how intertwined it is with our daily lives.

This site covers the basics of how the Internet came to be, how web browers started, how search engines started, the dot com bubble, and what the Internet is today. Click on the respective links located on the top right of the page to start learning or press the button to the right to get started!
